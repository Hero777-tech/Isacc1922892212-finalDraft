Explore Silchar and Assam

Silchar, nestled in the lush Barak Valley of Assam, is known for its vibrant culture and rich biodiversity. The region is home to several natural attractions and offers a unique blend of flora and fauna. Here are some must-visit places and things to see around Silchar:

Khaspur Ruins

Explore the ancient ruins of Khaspur, the historical capital of the Dimasa Kingdom.

Badarpur Fort

A historic fort offering a glimpse into the region's past, located near the Barak River.

Maniharan Tunnel

Considered sacred by the local people, this tunnel is associated with the legend of Lord Krishna.

Jalinga Tea Garden

A lush tea garden of Cachar, a serene place to experience the beauty of Assam's tea industry.

Kaziranga National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, home to the famous one-horned rhinoceros, located a few hours from Silchar.

Kakochang Waterfall

The waterfall is located about 13 km from the Bokakhat, a place in the Jorhat Region of Assam. Situated between the Kaziranga National Park and Bokakhat.

Doloo Lake

Dolu Lake is a serene lake located near Silchar in the Cachar District of Assam, India and is nestled amidst lush greenery, offering a tranquil getaway for nature lovers and peace seekers.

Son Beel

Shonbil is one of the largest lakes in southern Assam in India. This freshwater lake is situated in the Karimganj district, state of Assam.
